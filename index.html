MULTIDISCIPLINARY SOUND ARTIST

As a multidisciplinary sound artist and live performer, Dean Strasser, also known as Fonclair, explores the intersection of sound and visual art, crafting immersive auditory experiences that complement dynamic digital installations. Deeply rooted in sound system culture, Fonclair’s work blends finely tuned frequencies with raw, bass-driven energy. His soundscapes resonate in both body and mind, designed to fuse with cutting-edge visual art and create holistic sensory environments that go beyond traditional performances. On the dance floor, Fonclair’s sets are immersive journeys through rhythm and texture. Drawing from his Luxembourgish and Malagasy heritage, he weaves diverse influences into hypnotic soundscapes, building tension with precision and crafting deep, immersive atmospheres. A fixture at De Gudde Wëllen for over a decade, Fonclair is renowned for his technical mastery, his ability to read the crowd, and his focus on creating a shared experience. His performances emphasize the collective journey, bringing a raw, authentic energy that connects sound, space, and audience in a unique, lasting way.
