MULTIDISCIPLINARY SOUND ARTIST

As a multidisciplinary sound artist and live performer, Dean Strasser, also known as Fonclair, explores the intersection of sound and visual art, crafting immersive auditory experiences that complement dynamic digital installations. Pushing the boundaries of conventional sound design, he creates unique textures and atmospheres that bring art to life while constantly refining his technical skills and experimenting with new forms of expression. Rooted in a blend of Luxembourgish and Malagasy influences, Fonclair approaches his craft with an unwavering desire to grow and deepen his understanding of the relationship between sound and emotion. His Dj performances, are thoughtful mixes of rhythmes, melodies, and textures, that are designed to resonate with diverse audiences and create immersive experiences. A staple at De Gudde Wëllen for over a decade, he is known for his crazy technical moments, his knack for building tension, and his ability to read the crowd, ensuring each set feels authentic and, most of all, in the moment. His genuine love for music and openness to new ideas leave a lasting impression, focusing on creating a collective journey for listeners rather than seeking the spotlight.
